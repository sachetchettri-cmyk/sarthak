About

Behind the lens.

Sarthak Kunwar — also known online as Potato Creed — is a Sydney-based cinematographer, director, and editor with a love for natural light, patient framing, and stories that breathe.

He has shot prominent music videos for Nepali artists including Sagar Jung Kunwar's "Ankha Ma" and a Nepali rendition of "Dandelions." His portfolio spans indie narrative shorts, high-octane action for Freestyle Kings and Patriot Footbeds, event work for Bodega Collective, and documentary contributions for the UFC.
